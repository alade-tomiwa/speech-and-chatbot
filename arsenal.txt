Arsenal Football Club is a professional football club based in Islington, London, England.
Arsenal plays in the Premier League, the top flight of English football.
The club has won 13 league titles and a record 14 FA Cups.
Mikel Arteta is the current manager of the club.
The team plays their home matches at the Emirates Stadium.
The Emirates Stadium is located in Holloway, London.
Before 2006, Arsenal played at Highbury Stadium for 93 years.
Arsenal's main rivals are Tottenham Hotspur.
The match between Arsenal and Tottenham is referred to as the North London Derby.
Bukayo Saka is a key player for Arsenal and plays on the right wing.
Martin Odegaard is the current captain of Arsenal.
Thierry Henry is the club's all-time top goalscorer with 228 goals.
The club's traditional kit colors are red shirts with white sleeves.
Arsenal fans are often referred to as Gooners.
